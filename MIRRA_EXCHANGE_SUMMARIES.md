During extreme volatility (like a flash crash), OKX has a "security fund" to cover losses. If that fund runs out, OKX can take money from **profitable traders** to pay **losing traders**.

**Example**: You made $10,000 profit trading ETH futures. Market crashes. OKX's security fund is depleted. They take $2,000 of YOUR profit to cover someone else's $50,000 loss.
