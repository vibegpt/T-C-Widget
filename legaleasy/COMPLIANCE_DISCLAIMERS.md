## Risk Management

### Low-Risk Phrasing

**Use these patterns:**
- "This analysis found..."
- "You may want to review..."
- "Consider discussing with..."
- "Typical cost is... Your bill is..."
- "This clause means..."
- "Common vs. uncommon practice..."

### High-Risk Phrasing (Avoid)

**Never use:**
- "You should..."
- "I recommend..."
- "Don't..."
- "This is illegal/unfair/wrong..."
- "File a complaint..."
- "Refuse to pay..."

## Conclusion

**Key Takeaway**: Frame everything as **informational analysis**, not **tailored advice**.

**What you CAN do**:
- Analyze documents
- Identify potential issues
- Provide facts and comparisons
- Suggest professional consultation

**What you CANNOT do**:
- Tell users what to do
- Provide diagnoses or recommendations
- Give legal/medical/financial advice
- Create professional-client relationships

**Golden Rule**: When in doubt, add another disclaimer and suggest consulting a professional.
